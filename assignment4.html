QUESTION 1

By how many ways we can access elements in the DOM and write about them in brief?

ANSWER :

There are 5 ways to access elements in the DOM.
They are :

  1. getElementByID()
  2. getElementsByClassName()
  3. getElementsByTagName
  4. querySelector()
  5. querySelectorAll()
